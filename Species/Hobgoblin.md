## 🧬 Traits
*Copy these directly into the "Race Traits" section of your Character Sheet.*

### Race Traits
* **Martial Training:** you have +1 proficiency with all melee weapons 
* **Saving Face:** When you miss an attack roll or fail an ability check or saving throw, you can gain a bonus to the roll equal to the number of allies you can see within 30 feet (maximum bonus of +5). You can use this trait once per long rest.
* **Darkvision:** You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. In darkness, you see in shades of red.
