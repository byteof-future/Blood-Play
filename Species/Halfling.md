## 🧬 Traits
*Copy these directly into the "Race Traits" section of your Character Sheet.*

### Race Traits
* **Lucky:** When you roll a 1 on any attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can reroll the die and must use the new roll.
* **Brave:** You have advantage on saving throws against being frightened. Reduce duration of fear effects by 1 turn (minimum 1 turn).
* **Nimble Escape:** you can move through spaces occupied by creatures one size larger than you.
