## 🧬 Traits
*Copy these directly into the "Race Traits" section of your Character Sheet.*

### Race Traits
* **Gnome Cunning:** You have advantage on all Intelligence, Will, and Insight saving throws against magic. Additionally, reduce the duration of mental status effects (confusion, charm, fear) by 1 turn (minimum 1).
* **Artificer's Insight:** When using any crafted item, magical device, or technological contraption, gain +2 to all related checks. Can identify magical item properties with a successful Intelligence check (DC 15).
* **Tinker's Expertise:** Once per long rest, can repair broken non-magical equipment or create a small mechanical device that lasts until the end of your next long rest. Device examples: clockwork toy, fire starter, music box, or small tool (DM discretion on effects).
