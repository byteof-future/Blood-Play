## 🧬 Traits
*Copy these directly into the "Race Traits" section of your Character Sheet.*

### Race Traits
* **Dwarven Resilience:** Poison damage reduced by (4d6) + PC level. You have advantage on saving throws against poison and disease effects. Alcohol never impairs your abilities.
* **Stonecunning:** When in underground or stone structures, you have advantage on Perception and Ingenuity checks to notice hidden passages, traps, or structural weaknesses. You can automatically determine approximate depth underground and direction to the surface.
* **Master Craftsman:** When using or examining worked stone, metal, or crafted items, gain +3 to all related checks. Weapons and armor you craft gain +1 to AC or damage (DM discretion). You can repair damaged equipment with half the normal time and materials required.
