## 🧬 Traits
*Copy these directly into the "Race Traits" section of your Character Sheet.*

### Race Traits
* **Feline Agility:** Your reflexes and agility are unmatched. On your turn, you can move double your speed. Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you move 0 feet on one of your turns. Additionally, you take no damage from falls of 50 feet or less if you aren't incapacitated.
* **Cat's Claws:** Your claws are natural weapons dealing 1d4 x Strength Modifier slashing damage. You can make two claw attacks as an action. When you take the Attack action and hit with your claws, you can make an additional claw attack as a bonus action.
* **Hunter's Instinct:** You have advantage on Perception checks that rely on sight, hearing, or smell. In dim light or darkness, you can see twice as far, and you have advantage on Stealth checks. You always know the exact location of creatures within 10 feet, even when blinded.
