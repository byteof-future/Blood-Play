## 🧬 Traits
*Copy these directly into the "Race Traits" section of your Character Sheet.*

### Race Traits
* **Stone's Endurance:** Once per short rest, when you take damage, you can use your reaction to reduce the damage by 2 x RN. You can use this after seeing the damage but before applying it to your HP.
* **Powerful Build:** You have advantage on all strength rolls.
* **Mountain Born:** You have advantage on saving throws against environmental cold and altitude effects. You're naturally acclimated to high altitude and cold climates. Difficult terrain caused by ice, snow, or rocky ground doesn't cost you extra movement.
