## 🧬 Traits
*Copy these directly into the "Race Traits" section of your Character Sheet.*

### Race Traits
* **Natural Armor:** All incoming Piercing damage will be converted to blunt damage
* **Bite Attack:** Your fanged maw is a natural weapon dealing 1d6 + Strength Modifier piercing damage. On a critical hit, you can choose to grapple the target automatically (they must use an action to escape, contested Strength check).
* **Hold Breath:** You can hold your breath for up to 15 minutes. When swimming, your movement speed is doubled, and you gain advantage on Coordination checks related to aquatic movement or combat.
