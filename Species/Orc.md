## 🧬 Traits
*Copy these directly into the "Race Traits" section of your Character Sheet.*

### Race Traits
* **Relentless Endurance:** When reduced to 50% WHP, RN is increased by 50%.
* **Savage Attacks:** Will deal double damage every 3rd attack if the turns are only being used for attacking.
* **Mighty Build:** Gain +2 to Strength-based grapple checks.
