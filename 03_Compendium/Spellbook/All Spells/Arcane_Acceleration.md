## **Arcane Acceleration**

_Level 1 Alteration_

- CastingMethod:: Non-Verbal
- CastingTime:: Instant
- Range:: Self
- MPCost:: 10 MP
- Duration:: Instant

---

Description:: When I make a weapon attack, I can empower it with arcanic force. The attack will hit with extra 2d12 dmg (slash, piercing, cleave..upto the weapon). If I run up at least 10 feet before attacking then, extra dmg increases to 3d12.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Adds 2d12 (or 3d12 if charging) damage to a weapon attack.
- Damage Formula: (IM * (d12 + P)) + IS [Substat adjustment based on class]
