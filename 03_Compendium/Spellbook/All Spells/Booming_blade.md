## **Booming blade**

_Level 1 Energy_

- CastingMethod:: Non-Verbal
- CastingTime:: Instant
- Range:: Melee
- MPCost:: 30 MP
- Duration:: Instant

---

Description:: When a enemy gets hit, he will get 'charged'. If the enemy moves then he will get hit with thunder damage.

---

### **Mechanics**
- PrimaryEffect:: Charges enemy on hit; deals thunder damage if they move.
- Damage Formula: (IM * (d8 + P)) + IS [Substat adjustment based on class]
