# Description

Rust Monsters are strange aberrations that feed on metal. Their very touch causes metal to corrode and crumble to rust, making them the bane of armored warriors. They are not inherently aggressive but will attack if hungry or threatened.

These creatures have a unique sensory organ that allows them to detect metal from a distance, drawing them to armored prey. Their **Rust** trait means they deal double damage to any metallic objects, quickly destroying weapons, armor, and equipment.

Underground, they use **Tremor Sense** to detect movement, making them excellent ambush predators in caves and dungeons. Multiple Rust Monsters can work together, using their **Crackle** ability to cause structural collapse - a devastating environmental hazard.

The **Decay** ability makes their attacks progressively more dangerous, as each successive hit deals more damage as rust spreads across the victim's gear and exposed skin.

Adventurers who encounter Rust Monsters are wise to use wooden, stone, or magical weapons, as metal equipment will be quickly reduced to worthless rust.
