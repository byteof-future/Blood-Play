# Description

**Snake Minions** are servant creatures that serve The Naga or other powerful serpentine beings. They come in several varieties, each with different strengths and purposes:

## Types of Snake Minions

### Basic Snake
The most common type, small but numerous. They serve as scouts and swarm attackers, overwhelming prey with numbers rather than individual strength.

### Drifter
Faster and more aggressive than basic snakes, Drifters actively patrol territories and chase down fleeing prey. They have the highest strength among small snake types and deliver devastating bites.

### Snake swarm (Swarm)
These are colonies of tiny snakes that move and attack as a single writhing mass. While individually weak, their collective strength (STR 16) makes them formidable. They have balanced health pools and can overwhelm lone targets.

### Snake Guard (Large Guardian)
The elite protectors, these large serpents are stationed at important locations. With high Strength (16) and substantial Ward Health (200), they serve as formidable obstacles. Their powerful bite and ability to **Grapple** make them deadly in close combat.

## Combat Behavior

Snake Minions typically:
- Attack in coordinated groups
- Use terrain to their advantage (hiding in grass, dropping from trees)
- The S-Guard variant can **Grapple** enemies, causing Suffocation damage (15 dmg to CHP every other turn) and adding 1 fatigue per turn
- Protect their territory or master zealously

## Tactical Notes

- Swarms (Snake swarm) are vulnerable to area-effect attacks
- Snake Guards should be dealt with quickly due to their Grapple ability
- Drifters are fast and can prevent escape
- Basic Snakes are best handled with sweeping attacks

These creatures are most dangerous when serving under the command of The Naga, who can coordinate their attacks and use them strategically to wear down adventurers before the final confrontation.
