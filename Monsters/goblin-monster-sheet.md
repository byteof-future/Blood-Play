# Traits

**Mischief:** If 2 or more of your species are in sight (not mid-combat radius), your speed is doubled and Strength is increased by +2.

# Skills and Abilities

## Skills
Skills can be used once per long rest

1. **High Jump (Level 1):** They can jump 3× their length.

## Abilities
Abilities can be used the same times as Fight level per day

1. **[1] High Jump (1 AP):** Allows the goblin to jump 3× their body length.

2. **[2] Tantrum (1 AP):** Can make the species who are already of the same species attack (1×) again.

3. **[3] Maniac (1 AP):** Can use (2) Heavy melee attacks in one turn at the cost of (2) DHP.

# Description

Goblins are small, mischievous humanoids that live in tribes and prefer to overwhelm enemies with numbers. They are cunning when in groups, utilizing the **Mischief** trait to become faster and stronger when fighting alongside their kin.

**Adolescent Goblins** are young and inexperienced, using basic weapons and tactics.

**Adult Goblins** are more capable fighters, often equipped with better weapons like short bows or short swords.

**Boss/Alpha Goblins** are rare, powerful leaders who have mastered both physical combat and basic magic. They can use devastating heavy weapons and even cast fire spells. Their **Iron Body** ability makes them extremely durable, though it causes fatigue.

Goblins are most dangerous when they work together, using their **Tantrum** ability to coordinate attacks and their **Maniac** ability to unleash furious assault combinations at the cost of their own health.
