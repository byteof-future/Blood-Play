# Traits

**Keen Eye:** Has a vision of 200 ft. Can spot targets from extreme distances.

**Evasion:** Can evade most attacks if spells are 15 or higher. Extremely difficult to hit with magic.

**Concealment (Sky):** Has +5 in Stealth if it's 100 ft above. Nearly invisible from below when soaring.

# Description

**Thunder Birds** are among the most magnificent and dangerous creatures in existence. Found in the forests with huge trees of the Giant Forests of Black Sun region, they hunt medium to large-size creatures that are just below their carrying capacity. These massive avian predators are apex hunters that combine incredible physical power with innate magical abilities.

## Physical Characteristics

Thunder Birds are colossal in size. Their eggs alone are as big as a normal human, giving a sense of their tremendous scale. They are:
- **Super fast** - Capable of incredible aerial speeds
- **Master predators** - Can cross sound barriers, creating sonic booms
- **Highly perceptive** - Able to spot prey from 200 ft away with perfect clarity

## Behavior

Thunder Birds are not inherently aggressive but will fiercely defend their territory. They are:
- **Persistent hunters** - Once they target prey, they will chase it to the end of the earth
- **Usually solitary** - Alone hunters, but if nesting they may be seen with a partner or baby/egg
- **Territorial** - The eggs are as big as a normal human, and parents are extremely protective

## Combat Tactics

Thunder Birds are devastating in combat:

1. **Aerial Superiority:** They attack from above, using their **Concealment (Sky)** trait to remain hidden until striking
2. **Thunder Drop:** Their signature move - grabbing prey and dropping it from lethal heights
3. **Crowd Control:** **Instill Fear** and **Piercing Screech** disable multiple opponents
4. **Environment Manipulation:** **Darkness Helm** creates tactical advantage
5. **Evasion:** Their magical resistance (Evasion trait) makes them nearly immune to high-level spells

## Developmental Stages

**Chick:** Even young Thunder Birds are incredibly strong (STR 100) but fragile (low CHP). They can already pick up medium creatures but lack the full magical abilities of adults.

**Adult (Giant Forests Black Sun):** Fully mature Thunder Birds have balanced stats with massive health pools, devastating attacks, and complete mastery of their abilities.

## Warnings for Adventurers

- Never approach a nest - parent Thunder Birds will fight to the death
- If you hear a sonic boom, take cover immediately
- Their **Thunder Drop** ability is often fatal - avoid being grabbed at all costs
- High-level spells often fail due to Evasion - rely on physical attacks
- If darkness suddenly falls in an open area, a Thunder Bird is likely attacking

Thunder Birds command respect and fear throughout the lands. They are living storms, embodiments of thunder and sky, and few who challenge them live to tell the tale.
