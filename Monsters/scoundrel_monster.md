# Traits

**Pack Tactics** - Scoundrels gain advantage on attack rolls when an ally is within 5 feet of the target. They fight more bravely and effectively in groups.

**Nimble Escape** - As a bonus action, can Disengage or Hide. Scoundrels are masters of hit-and-run tactics, striking then retreating to safety.

**Cowardly** - When reduced below 50% HP or when all allies are defeated, must make Will save (DC 12) or become frightened and attempt to flee on their next turn.

# Description

Scoundrels are small, wiry goblinoid creatures standing about 3-4 feet tall with mottled greenish-gray skin and large, pointed ears. Their eyes gleam with cunning mischief, and their nimble fingers are always twitching, ready to snatch, steal, or stab. They dress in patchwork clothing made from stolen scraps and smell of mud and mildew.

Individually weak and cowardly, Scoundrels rely on numbers, ambush tactics, and hit-and-run strikes to survive. They're surprisingly intelligent for their size, capable of setting crude traps and coordinating group attacks. Young Scoundrels are more playful and curious, often getting into trouble through mischief rather than malice. Adolescents are bolder and more aggressive, eager to prove themselves. Adults are the craftiest, using every dirty trick they know to gain an advantage.

Scoundrels live in loose tribal groups led by the strongest or smartest among them. They make their homes in caves, ruins, sewers, or abandoned buildings, creating warrens of tunnels and hiding spots. They're opportunistic scavengers and thieves, raiding farms and travelers for food and shiny objects.

**Tactics:** 
- **Young:** Run away or call for help if confronted. Only fight if cornered or in large groups.
- **Adolescent:** Use High Jump to gain superior positions, then pelt enemies with ranged attacks. Retreat if taking heavy damage.
- **Adult:** Start combat hidden, using Stealth for ambushes. Focus ranged attacks on spellcasters, then use Distract and pack tactics to overwhelm isolated targets. Adults flee if reduced below 50% HP unless protecting their young.

**Group Tactics:** Scoundrels always fight in groups if possible:
- 3-6 Young with 1 Adolescent overseer
- 2-4 Adolescents with 1 Adult leader
- 4-8 mixed groups for raiding parties

**Habitat:** Underground warrens, forest caves, ruined buildings, sewer systems, any place dark and defensible near civilization.

**Encounter Notes:**
- **FL 1 encounter:** 4-6 Young Scoundrels
- **FL 2 encounter:** 2-3 Adolescent Scoundrels  
- **FL 3 encounter:** 1 Adult + 2 Adolescents OR 1 Adult + 4-6 Young
- Scale difficulty by adding more Scoundrels or mixing age groups
