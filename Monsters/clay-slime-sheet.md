# Description

**Clay Slimes** are among the most dangerous and tenacious hunters in existence. These ooze-like creatures are masters of deception, capable of transforming into the last thing they have seen - whether object or creature. Their transformations are nearly perfect, copying not just appearance but also the body mass, abilities, and even mannerisms of their target.

## Abilities & Behavior

The most terrifying aspect of Clay Slimes is their ability to perfectly mimic creatures. They can:
- Transform into any object or creature similar to their body mass
- Perform basic functions that creatures or objects would normally do (like opening a chest if transformed into a key)
- **Crucially:** They always keep their mouth shut to conceal their fangs, which are the only telltale sign of their true nature

Their **Camouflage** trait makes them nearly undetectable at range, and their **Proficient** skill means they wield the abilities of their chosen form with surprising competence - automatically having Level 2 proficiency with everything their form can do.

## Combat Strategy

Clay Slimes are ambush predators. They wait in plain sight, disguised as harmless objects or familiar creatures, then strike when prey comes close. In dire situations, multiple Clay Slimes can **Merge** together, combining their stats into a single more powerful entity - though this comes at the cost of losing their specialized traits and proficiencies.

## Warning Signs

Adventurers should watch for:
- Creatures or companions that never open their mouths
- Objects that seem slightly "off" or in unusual positions
- A faint clay-like smell
- Lack of breathing or other subtle life signs

Clay Slimes represent the ultimate shapeshifter threat - you never know if the chest you're opening, the companion beside you, or the weapon you're reaching for is actually a hungry predator waiting to strike.
