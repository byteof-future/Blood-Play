# Traits

**Command Weak** - Can command up to 3 monsters below its Fight Level. Commanded monsters act on the Chimera's initiative and follow its mental orders within 120 feet.

**Life Debt** - When CHP drops below 100, the Chimera consumes its own life span to enter a berserker state. Gains +15 Strength and +12 Agility for 5 rounds, but loses 20 WHP per round while active. This state ends when WHP reaches 0 or CHP is restored above 100.

**Multi-Headed** - The Chimera has three heads (lion, goat, serpent). Can make attacks from all three heads in a single turn. If one head is disabled (takes 50+ damage in one hit), loses access to that head's attack but other heads remain functional.

# Skills and Abilities

## Skills
Skills can be used once per long rest

1. **Soul Vase** - The Chimera creates 3 magical soul vessels (spectral orbs) that orbit around it. Each vessel increases its Resistance by +3 (total +9 RES, RN becomes 70). Each vessel has 30 HP and can be targeted separately. Vessels last until destroyed or 10 minutes.

## Abilities
Abilities can be used 8 times per day (= Fight Level)

1. **Regeneration** - Can regenerate 20% of its maximum WHP (100 HP). Takes 1 full turn to complete. Cannot be interrupted, but Chimera cannot attack while regenerating.

2. **Chaos Core** - Unleashes chaotic energy attacking all enemies on the battlefield. Primary target (closest enemy) takes full damage. All other enemies take half damage. Uses one ability charge.

3. **Terror** - All creatures within 30 feet must make Intimidation check (DC = 8 + Chimera's Intimidation [8] = DC 16) or be frightened, unable to attack for 1 turn. Affected creatures can only move away or dodge. Can repeat save at end of each turn.

# Description

The Chimera is a terrifying hybrid monstrosity combining the features of multiple beasts into one horrifying whole. Its muscular leonine body stands 10 feet tall at the shoulder, covered in tawny fur that bristles with latent rage. Three heads crown this nightmare: a savage lion's head with a mane of flames, a horned goat's head that breathes blistering fire, and a serpentine dragon head on a long, whip-like tail.

Born from dark magic or twisted experiments, Chimeras are intelligent enough to command lesser beasts and cruel enough to enjoy their dominion. They often serve as guardians of ancient ruins or masters of monster-infested territories. Their multi-headed nature makes them unpredictable in combat—one moment savage and bestial, the next calculating and cunning.

**Tactics:** The Chimera typically opens combat with Fireball to soften grouped enemies, then charges into melee range. It focuses its physical attacks on the strongest-looking opponent while using its tail to strike at casters or archers. When bloodied (below 50% HP), it may use Terror to scatter enemies before activating Life Debt for a devastating final assault. Smart Chimeras use Soul Vase before major battles.

**Habitat:** Mountains, ancient ruins, magical wastelands, or anywhere monsters gather. Often claims a lair in a cave system or abandoned fortress.

**Encounter Notes:** This is a FL 8 boss monster suitable for a party of 4-5 level 8-10 characters. The fight becomes significantly harder if the Chimera has minions or uses terrain to its advantage.
