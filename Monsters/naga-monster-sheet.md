# Description

**The Naga** is a ferocious beast created to guard and protect. It uses its sleeping aura to catch escaping prey, often guarding treasure for a being far more powerful than itself. The Naga's most distinctive feature is a magical crystal embedded in its chest, which often contains a key to whatever it is guarding. When this crystal is broken, the Naga's magical resistance is nullified.

## Physical Description

Standing 25 ft tall when reared up and stretching to a full length of 100 ft, the Naga is a serpentine monstrosity of immense proportions. Its scaled body is incredibly durable, with Ward Health representing its thick hide and Core Health representing its vital organs. The glowing crystal on its chest pulses with magical energy.

## Behavior & Tactics

Nagas are patient hunters and dedicated guardians. They typically:
- Remain coiled and dormant until prey approaches
- Use **Sleep Aura** to incapacitate multiple targets simultaneously
- Command lesser creatures to do their bidding
- Employ **Yawn** to instill fear and hallucinations in those who resist sleep
- Use **Tremor** to cause environmental hazards and prevent escape
- Deploy **Spray** poison attack when facing persistent threats

## Combat Strategy

The Naga is most dangerous in its lair. Its massive size allows it to control the battlefield, while its **Sleep Aura** can neutralize entire parties. The **Tremor** ability can cause cave-ins or structural collapse, adding environmental danger to the fight.

The crystal on its chest (RN: 75, 250 HP) must be destroyed to fully defeat the Naga, as it provides both magical protection and often serves as the key to whatever treasure or secret it guards.

## Weaknesses

- Low Intelligence makes it susceptible to clever tactics
- Moderate Resistance means it's vulnerable to sustained attacks
- The chest crystal is both its strength and weakness
- Limited ranged capabilities beyond its spray attack

Adventurers who face a Naga should prepare for a battle of attrition, bring ways to resist sleep effects, and target the crystal to break its magical defenses. Few survive an encounter with this legendary guardian beast.
