# Description

**Crocodiles** are ancient reptilian predators that have perfected the art of ambush hunting. They are most commonly found in rivers, swamps, marshes, and coastal areas where they can use their natural camouflage to devastating effect.

## Physical Characteristics

Crocodiles are heavily armored reptiles with:
- **Thick scaled hide** providing substantial Ward Health
- **Powerful jaws** capable of crushing bone
- **Muscular tail** used for swimming and combat
- **Low profile** making them difficult to spot in water

## Behavior & Hunting Strategy

Crocodiles are patient ambush predators:
- **Stealth hunters** (Stealth 5) - virtually invisible in murky water
- **Wait for prey** to come to the water's edge
- **Explosive bursts** of speed when attacking
- **Territorial** - will defend their hunting grounds aggressively

## The Death Grip

The most feared ability of crocodiles is their **Death Grip**. When they catch prey in their jaws:
1. If in water, they apply (2) cling damage continuously
2. The victim is dragged underwater
3. Drowning becomes a serious threat
4. Escape becomes extremely difficult

Alpha crocodiles can perform the devastating **Death Roll** - spinning violently while gripping their prey, dealing catastrophic damage and completely disorienting the victim.

## Combat Tactics

Crocodiles employ simple but effective tactics:
1. **Remain hidden** in water using natural camouflage
2. **Wait for opportunity** - prey drinking, crossing water, or near shore
3. **Lightning strike** - explosive lunge from water
4. **Bite and grip** - latch onto prey with powerful jaws
5. **Drag underwater** - use Death Grip to drown and finish prey

## Variant Types

**Normal Crocodile:** Common in most waterways. Dangerous but manageable for prepared adventurers.

**Alpha/Giant Crocodile:** Massive specimens that have survived for decades. Much higher stats, larger size, and more aggressive. These ancient predators may have small treasure hoards from previous victims.

## Survival Tips

When in crocodile territory:
- **Avoid water edges** especially at dawn and dusk
- **Look for ripples** and disturbances in the water
- **Never swim** in unknown waters
- **If grabbed**, focus all effort on breaking the grip before being dragged under
- **Stay out of water** when fighting crocodiles - they lose their Death Grip advantage on land

Crocodiles are not the most intelligent creatures, but they don't need to be. Millions of years of evolution have made them perfect killing machines in their aquatic domains. Respect the water, or become prey.
