# Campaign Folder Structure

This structure aims to separate "Session Management" (what you need *during* play) from "World Building" (Lore/Background) and "Mechanics" (Rules/Reference).
=
## Folder Purpose & Organization

### 00_Sessions (Active Play)
*Contains everything you need quickly during a session.*
- `Current_Session.md`: Quick reference for the upcoming game.
- `Session_Logs/`: Recaps of past games (moved from `WorldBuilding/Sessions story so far`).
- `Future_Plans/`: Notes for upcoming arcs (moved from `WorldBuilding/Sessions future plan`).

### 01_Characters (People)
*Unified directory for all individuals.*
- `PCs/`: Player Character folders (moved from `Playercharacters`).
- `NPCs/`:
    - `Allies/`: Important friendly NPCs.
    - `Villains/`: Major antagonists.
    - `Gods/`: Deities and powerful entities (moved from `Characters/NPC's/God's`).
    - `Factions/`: Organizations and groups.

### 02_World_Amanac (Lore & Geography)
*The "Lore Bible" of your setting.*
- `Atlas/`: Maps and locations.
    - `Cosmology/`: The 7 Layers (Zenith, Nirvana, etc.).
    - `Vivestera/`: Continents, Cities, Points of Interest.
- `History/`: Timelines, Myths, Legends.
- `Magic_&_Religion/`: How the magic system works, religious practices.

### 03_Compendium (Mechanics)
*Rules, stat blocks, and items.*
- `Bestiary/`: Monster stat blocks (moved from `MonsterArchive`).
- `Spellbook/`: Spells and magical effects (moved from `Spells`).
- `Items/`: Magic items, loot, equipment lists.
- `Rules/`: House rules, combat helpers (moved from `Battle Helpers`).

### 04_Assets (Files)
*Images, templates, and other resources.*
- `Images/`: Maps, character art, tokens.
- `Templates/`: Markdown templates for new sessions, NPCs, locations.
