## **Status Effect Details**

### **Bleeding**
- Light: 1d4 damage per turn, stops after combat or DC 12 Medicine check
- Severe: 1d6 damage per turn, DC 15 Medicine check or magical healing required

### **Ablaze**
- Takes 1d6 fire damage per turn
- Can be extinguished with Reflex DC 15 (roll, drop, use water)
- Spreads to flammable objects within 5ft

### **Poisoned**
- -2 penalty to attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws
- Takes ongoing poison damage as specified by source
- Duration varies by poison type

### **Paralyzed**
- Cannot move or take actions
- Auto-fail Strength and Agility saves
- Attacks against paralyzed creatures have advantage

### **Confused**
- Rolls 1d4 each turn: 1=attack self, 2-3=do nothing, 4=attack random creature
- Can attempt Will save DC 15 at end of each turn to end effect

### **Frozen**
- Agility and Reflex reduced by specified amount
- Movement speed halved
- If reduced to 0 HP while frozen, shatters (instant death)

### **Stunned**
- Cannot take actions or reactions
- Auto-fail Reflex and Agility saves
- Duration typically 1 turn unless specified otherwise
