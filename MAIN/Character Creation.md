# [[SpeciesIndex]]
This is the first choice you need to make while making a character you need to choose what will be the species of your Character you can choose from already given species or make your own.

Your species gives the base stats of your character 

You also get [[Traits]] based on your species. There are two kind of traits the Basic Traits and Specific traits. Basic traits are the ones you don't have to choose and will always be at your disposal while the Specific traits are the ones you have to choose and may or may not have access to at all times.

# [[OriginIndex]]
Now you have to think about how your character came to be based on this you will get stats and in a few cases a Curse or a Boon as well depending on your character.

# [[BackgroundIndex]]
This is where we talk about how your character has lived so far this also gives you stats.
