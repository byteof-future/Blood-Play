# Marsh Elder (*Alnus glutinosa*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WETLANDS_&_RIPARIAN_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Height: 10-20 meters
- Ecology: Nitrogen-fixing, improves poor soils
- Wood: Water-resistant, used for underwater pilings
- Cultural: Associated with water spirits
