# Water Lily (*Nymphaea alba*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WETLANDS_&_RIPARIAN_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Flowers: Large, white, fragrant, float on surface
- Leaves: Round, floating pads
- Roots: Anchored in mud, edible (starchy)
- Cultural: Symbol of purity, featured in art
