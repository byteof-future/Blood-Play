# Swamp Willow (*Salix palustris*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WETLANDS_&_RIPARIAN_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Height: 8-15 meters, often multi-trunked
- Roots: Extensive, stabilize shorelines
- Bark: Contains salicin (natural aspirin)
- Uses: Flexible branches (basketry), medicine (pain, fever)
