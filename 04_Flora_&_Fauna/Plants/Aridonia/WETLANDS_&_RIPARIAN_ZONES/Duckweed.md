# Duckweed (*Lemna minor*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WETLANDS_&_RIPARIAN_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Size: Tiny, 2-3mm, floats on surface
- Growth: Rapid, can cover entire ponds
- Ecology: Food for waterfowl, fish
- Uses: Edible (high protein), water filtration
