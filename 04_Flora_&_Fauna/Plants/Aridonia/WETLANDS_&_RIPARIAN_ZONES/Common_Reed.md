# Common Reed (*Phragmites australis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WETLANDS_&_RIPARIAN_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Height: 2-4 meters, forms dense stands
- Spread: Aggressive, rhizomatous, can dominate
- Uses: Thatching, arrow shafts, basketry, erosion control
- Ecology: Provides nesting habitat for marsh birds
