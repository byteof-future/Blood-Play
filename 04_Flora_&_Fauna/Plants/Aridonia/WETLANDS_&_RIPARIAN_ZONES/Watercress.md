# Watercress (*Nasturtium officinale*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WETLANDS_&_RIPARIAN_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Habitat: Cold, flowing water
- Taste: Peppery, tangy
- Uses: Salad green, high in vitamins
- Caution: Can harbor parasites if wild-harvested from contaminated water
