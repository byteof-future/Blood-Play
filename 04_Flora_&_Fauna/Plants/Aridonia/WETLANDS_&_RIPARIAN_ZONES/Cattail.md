# Cattail (*Typha latifolia*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WETLANDS_&_RIPARIAN_ZONES


### Additional Info
- Height: 1.5-3 meters
- Seedhead: Brown, cylindrical, explosive dispersal
- Uses: Every part edible (roots, shoots, pollen, seeds)
- Fiber: Leaves for weaving, down for insulation
- Cultural: "Supermarket of the swamp" - critical survival food
