# Ghostmoss (*Bryophyta spectrus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Ubiquitous in deep forests, particularly Sprout Kingdom
- **Characteristics:** Luminescent green moss that grows on north-facing tree trunks
- **Magical Properties:** Glows in darkness, intensity increases near ley lines
- **Cultural Use:** Navigation aid, harvested for lanterns that need no flame
- **Alchemy:** Key ingredient in night-vision potions
