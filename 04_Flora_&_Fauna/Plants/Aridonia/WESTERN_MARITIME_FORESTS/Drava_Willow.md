# Drava Willow (*Salix dravicus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** All major waterways in western regions
- **Height:** 8-15 meters
- **Form:** Weeping habit, branches drape to ground
- **Bark:** Gray-green, deeply fissured
- **Leaves:** Long, narrow, silver-green
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Requires permanent water, stabilizes riverbanks

### Additional Info
- Basket weaving (FylloDraya famous for willow crafts)
- Bark contains pain-relieving compounds
- Young shoots fed to livestock
