# Giant Wetfern (*Filixgigantea palustris*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Height: 15-20 meters (tree-sized fern)
- Structure: Fibrous trunk with massive fronds at top
- Ecology: Thrives in permanently wet soils, creates unique microhabitat
- Uses: Young fronds edible (fiddleheads), trunk fiber for rope
- Distribution: City of BitterMarsh, wetland edges
