# Thornshield Holly (*Ilex spinaegis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Defense: Dense thorny branches protect forest floor
- Berries: Red, mildly toxic to humans, important winter bird food
- Uses: Living fences, decorative (winter berries)
