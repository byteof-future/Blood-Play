# Debrica Fern (*Pteridium Debrica*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Abundant in Kingdom of Debrica, common elsewhere
- **Height:** 60-120cm
- **Fronds:** Broad, triangular, bright green
- **Spores:** Release in autumn, golden clouds
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Indicates good forest soil, can become invasive

### Additional Info
- Young fronds (fiddleheads) eaten as vegetable
- Dried fronds used as animal bedding
- Traditional thatching material
