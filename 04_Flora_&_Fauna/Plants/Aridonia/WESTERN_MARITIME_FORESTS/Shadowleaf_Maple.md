# Shadowleaf Maple (*Acer umbrosum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Characteristic: Large palmate leaves capture limited understory light
- Ecology: Tolerates deep shade, produces sweet sap in spring
- Uses: Sap for sweetener, wood for musical instruments
- Fall Color: Deep purple-red
