# Rust-Cap Mushroom (*Agaricus ferrugineus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Only in Rust Ironwood groves
- **Cap:** 5-15cm, rust-red color
- **Season:** Autumn, after first rains
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Mycorrhizal with Rust Ironwood roots

### Additional Info
- Edible, prized delicacy in Kingdom of Rust
- Expensive trade good
- Said to taste of forest floor and iron
