# Rust Ironwood (*Ferroxylon oxidans*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Northern major island, heavily logged
- **Height:** 40-45 meters
- **Trunk Diameter:** 1.5-3 meters
- **Lifespan:** 300-600 years
- **Bark:** Russet-red with metallic sheen, extremely dense
- **Leaves:** Small, needle-like, rust-colored in autumn
- **Unique Property:** Wood contains iron compounds, rusts when cut
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Slow-growing, poor fire tolerance

### Additional Info
- Kingdom of Rust derives its name from this tree
- Historically used for armor, tools (now depleted)
- Sawdust used in alchemical preparations
