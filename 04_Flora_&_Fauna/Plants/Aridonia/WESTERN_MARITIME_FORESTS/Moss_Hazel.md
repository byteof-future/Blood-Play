# Moss Hazel (*Corylus muscosa*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Size: 5-8 meters, multi-stemmed
- Nuts: Edible, important food source for wildlife and humans
- Bark: Completely covered in moss and epiphytes
- Uses: Nuts (oil, food), flexible wood (baskets)
