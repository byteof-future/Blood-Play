# Strangler Vine (*Ficus strangulatus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** All old-growth maritime forests
- **Form:** Begins as epiphyte, grows down to soil
- **Habit:** Eventually strangles and kills host tree
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Critical to forest regeneration (creates gaps)

### Additional Info
- Metaphor for toxic relationships in literature
- Wood: twisted, decorative after host dies
