# Fyllo Oak (*Quercus cyellicus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Throughout western islands except extreme northern BitterMarsh
- **Height:** 35-50 meters
- **Trunk Diameter:** 2-4 meters
- **Lifespan:** 500-800 years
- **Bark:** Deeply furrowed, dark gray-brown, resistant to fire
- **Leaves:** Broad, leathery, dark green with silver undersides
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Dominant canopy species, supports 200+ dependent species

### Additional Info
- Primary shipbuilding timber (City of FylloDraya)
- Acorns ground into flour during lean times
- Sacred to druidic orders in Sprout Kingdom
