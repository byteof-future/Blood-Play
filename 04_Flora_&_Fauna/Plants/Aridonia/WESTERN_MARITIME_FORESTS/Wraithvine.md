# Wraithvine (*Hedera mortuum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Common in ruins (Prauka, Solivirum), avoided in living forests
- **Characteristics:** Gray-green vine with translucent leaves, produces white flowers
- **Magical Properties:** Feeds on ambient death magic; grows aggressively in battlefields
- **Danger Level:** Low, but presence indicates dark magic residue
- **Length:** 10-30 meters
- **Cultural Use:** Necromancers use it as reagent; others burn it to cleanse areas
