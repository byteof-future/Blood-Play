# Storm Pine (*Pinus tempestus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Exposed coastal areas, all western islands
- **Height:** 30-40 meters
- **Trunk Diameter:** 1-2 meters
- **Lifespan:** 200-400 years
- **Bark:** Gray-brown, deeply ridged, wind-resistant
- **Needles:** Long (15-25cm), flexible, in clusters of 5
- **Cones:** Large (20-30cm), take 3 years to mature
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Tolerates salt spray, coastal exposure

### Additional Info
- Resin for ship waterproofing
- Lumber for masts and spars
- Pine nuts harvested and traded
