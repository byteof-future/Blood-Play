# Ghost Bracket (*Ganoderma phantasmatis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Size:** 20-40cm diameter
- **Distribution:** Old-growth forests, especially Sprout Kingdom
- **Color:** Pure white, luminescent in darkness
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Decomposes dead fyllo Oak

### Additional Info
- Medicinal (immune system booster)
- Decorative (dried brackets last for years)
- Used in Sprout Kingdom spiritual ceremonies
