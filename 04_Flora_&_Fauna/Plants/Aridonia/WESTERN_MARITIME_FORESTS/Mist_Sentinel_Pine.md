# Mist Sentinel Pine (*Pinus maritimus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Coastal zones, particularly BitterMarsh and western Westallis
- **Characteristics:** Straight trunks, branches only in upper third, deep tap roots
- **Magical Properties:** Needles glow faintly blue in presence of strong magic
- **Height:** 40-60 meters
- **Lifespan:** 600-1,200 years
- **Cultural Use:** Primary shipbuilding timber, masts for magical vessels
- **Wood Properties:** Flexible, salt-resistant, naturally waterproofed
