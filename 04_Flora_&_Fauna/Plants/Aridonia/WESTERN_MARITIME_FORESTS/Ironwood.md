# Ironwood (*Ferrosylva maritima*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Height: 25-30 meters
- Bark: Dark gray-black, extremely dense
- Wood Properties: Sinks in water, takes decades to decay
- Ecology: Grows slowly, lives 500+ years
- Uses: Tool handles, weapons, high-value trade commodity
- Distribution: Dwarven City of Ayarson mountain forests
