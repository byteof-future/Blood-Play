# Bloodthorn Bush (*Crataegus sanguineus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Forest edges, particularly near ruins like Prauka
- **Characteristics:** Dense thorny growth, deep red berries, thorns ooze red sap
- **Magical Properties:** Berries enhance blood magic, sap can be used in binding rituals
- **Danger Level:** Moderate - thorns cause festering wounds
- **Height:** 2-4 meters
- **Cultural Use:** Vorthic blood mages cultivate it; western kingdoms consider it evil
