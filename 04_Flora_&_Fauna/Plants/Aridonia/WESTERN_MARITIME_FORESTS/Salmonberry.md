# Salmonberry (*Rubus salmonensis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Fruit: Orange-pink, salmon-colored, edible
- Growth: Forms dense thickets along streams
- Ecology: Important bear and bird food
- Uses: Jams, fresh eating, medicinal (leaves for tea)
