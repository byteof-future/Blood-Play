# Cerulea Blue-Star (*Stellaria ceruleana*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Throughout Cerulea and southern islands
- **Height:** 15-30cm
- **Flowers:** Five-petaled stars, brilliant cerulean blue
- **Blooming:** Early spring (February-April)
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Carpet-forms in mature forests

### Additional Info
- Kingdom of Cerulea's heraldic flower
- Dye source (blue pigment)
- Romantic symbolism (given between lovers)
