# Sproutking Cedar (*Thuja gigantea*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Endemic to Sprout Kingdom, gives the island its name
- **Characteristics:** Enormous buttressed roots, reddish bark, grows in groves
- **Magical Properties:** Roots create underground mycelial network that can transmit simple messages
- **Height:** 50-70 meters
- **Lifespan:** 1,000+ years
- **Cultural Use:** Sacred to Sylvanic druids, never harvested by natives
- **Wood Properties:** Aromatic, insect-repelling, extremely rot-resistant
- **Notes:** Largest specimen "The Patriarch" is 82 meters tall, 14 meters diameter
