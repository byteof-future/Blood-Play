# Emerald Carpet Fern (*Pteridium viridium*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Forest floor dominant species (60% coverage in mature forests)
- **Characteristics:** Dense growth, fronds remain green year-round
- **Height:** 0.3-1 meter
- **Wildlife:** Provides cover for small game, deer bed in it
- **Cultural Use:** Young fronds (fiddleheads) are edible, considered a delicacy
