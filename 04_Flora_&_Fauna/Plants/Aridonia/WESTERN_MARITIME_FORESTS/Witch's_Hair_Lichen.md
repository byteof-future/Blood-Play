# Witch's Hair Lichen (*Usnea magus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Densest in Sprout Kingdom's oldest groves
- **Characteristics:** Gray-green strands hang from branches in dense forests
- **Magical Properties:** Absorbs ambient magic slowly; old growth can store minor spells
- **Length:** 10-50 cm hanging strands
- **Cultural Use:** Sylvanic mages harvest carefully for spell components
- **Indicator Species:** Shows forest health and magic saturation
