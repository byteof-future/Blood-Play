# Witch's Broom (*Salix mysticus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Form: Twisted, gnarled branches
- Folklore: Believed to ward off evil spirits
- Ecology: Grows in disturbed areas, pioneer species
- Uses: Wand-making, basket weaving, divination rituals
