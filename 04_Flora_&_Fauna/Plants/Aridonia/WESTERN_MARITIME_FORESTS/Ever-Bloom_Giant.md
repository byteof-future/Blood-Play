# Ever-Bloom Giant (*Sequoia semperflorens*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Sprout Kingdom exclusively, never transplanted successfully
- **Height:** 60-85 meters
- **Trunk Diameter:** 4-8 meters
- **Age:** Up to 2000 years
- **Bark:** Red-brown, fire-resistant (spongy)
- **Leaves:** Scale-like, evergreen
- **Cones:** Small (2-3cm), year-round production
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Supports aerial gardens in canopy

### Additional Info
- Sacred to Sprout Kingdom druids
- Logging strictly forbidden (death penalty)
- Hollows in trunks used as shrines
- Bark: medicine for various ailments
