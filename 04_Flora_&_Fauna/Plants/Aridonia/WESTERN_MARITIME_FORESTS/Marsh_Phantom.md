# Marsh Phantom (*Phantasmus palustris*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** BitterMarsh wetlands, rare elsewhere
- **Height:** 30-60cm
- **Form:** Translucent white flowers on thin stalks
- **Blooming:** Night-blooming, glows faintly in darkness
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Carnivorous, traps small insects

### Additional Info
- Used in BitterMarsh to mark safe paths through swamps
- Alchemical ingredient (luminescence properties)
- Featured in ghost stories and folklore
