# Veilwood Oak (*Quercus nebulosus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Dominates Kingdom of Rust and northern Cerulea
- **Characteristics:** Massive spreading canopy, bark produces natural fog-attracting oils
- **Magical Properties:** Bark can store ambient magic for 24 hours
- **Height:** 35-50 meters
- **Lifespan:** 400-800 years
- **Cultural Use:** Sacred to Rustic culture, used in industrial magic-tech
- **Wood Properties:** Extremely dense, resists rot, slightly magic-conductive
