# Silverthread Moss (*Bryophytum argenteum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Coverage: Blankets forest floor, tree trunks, rocks
- Appearance: Silver-green, luminescent in moonlight
- Water Retention: Holds up to 20x its weight in water
- Uses: Wound dressing, water filtration, insulation
