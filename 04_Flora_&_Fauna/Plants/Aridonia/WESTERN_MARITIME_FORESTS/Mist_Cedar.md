# Mist Cedar (*Cedropsis nebulosa*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Height: 35-45 meters, straight trunk
- Bark: Reddish-brown, fibrous, naturally fire-resistant
- Needles: Blue-green, aromatic, retain moisture
- Ecology: Creates acidic soil conditions, dominates older forests
- Uses: Construction timber, aromatic oils, medicinal (respiratory)
- Distribution: Western archipelago, particularly coastal ranges
