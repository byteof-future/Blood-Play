# Silverleaf Birch (*Betula argentum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Understory throughout western forests, particularly abundant in Debrica
- **Characteristics:** Pure white bark that literally reflects light like silver
- **Magical Properties:** Bark can reflect minor illusion spells
- **Height:** 15-25 meters
- **Lifespan:** 80-150 years
- **Cultural Use:** Bark used for magical scroll-making, wood for staffs
- **Wood Properties:** Light, easily worked, holds enchantments well
