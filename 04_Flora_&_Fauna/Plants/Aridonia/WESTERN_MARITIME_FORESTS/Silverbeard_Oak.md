# Silverbeard Oak (*Quercus argentalis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Height: 30-40 meters
- Bark: Smooth, silvery-gray with lichen growth giving "bearded" appearance
- Leaves: Large, leathery, dark green with silver undersides
- Ecology: Keystone species, provides nesting sites for dozens of bird species
- Uses: Ship timber (water-resistant), royal furniture, sacred groves
- Distribution: Most common in Sprout Kingdom interior
