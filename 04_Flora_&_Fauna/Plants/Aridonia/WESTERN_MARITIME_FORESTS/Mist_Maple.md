# Mist Maple (*Acer nebulosus*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Throughout western forests, especially river valleys
- **Height:** 10-20 meters
- **Leaves:** 7-lobed, turn brilliant crimson and gold in autumn
- **Bark:** Smooth, pale gray
- **Sap:** Clear, sweet, can be tapped for syrup
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Shade-tolerant, prefers moist soils

### Additional Info
- Syrup production (especially Kingdom of Debrica)
- Wood used for fine furniture, instruments
- Leaves used in decorative arts
