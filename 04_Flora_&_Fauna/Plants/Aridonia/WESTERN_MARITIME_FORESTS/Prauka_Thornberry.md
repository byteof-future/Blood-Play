# Prauka Thornberry (*Rubus praukensis*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Forest edges, clearings, particularly around Ruins of Prauka
- **Height:** 1-3 meters
- **Form:** Dense, thorny thickets
- **Thorns:** 2-4cm long, slightly curved
- **Berries:** Dark purple, tart, high in vitamin C
- **Flowering:** White flowers in late spring
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Colonizes disturbed areas, provides wildlife food

### Additional Info
- Berry preservation for winter (jams, dried)
- Thickets used as living fences
- Medicinal: leaf tea for digestive issues
