# Moss-Oak (*Quercus muscosum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Sprout Kingdom, wettest areas
- **Height:** 25-40 meters
- **Appearance:** Entirely covered in moss, ferns, epiphytes
- **Leaves:** Barely visible under epiphytes
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Host to 50+ epiphytic species

### Additional Info
- Epiphyte gardens (medicinal plants)
- Wood: unusable (too water-logged)
