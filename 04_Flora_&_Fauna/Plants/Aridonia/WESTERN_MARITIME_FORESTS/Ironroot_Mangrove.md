# Ironroot Mangrove (*Rhizophora ferrum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Coastal swamps of BitterMarsh, northern Prauka shores
- **Characteristics:** Dark brown-black bark, aerial roots extract minerals from water
- **Magical Properties:** Roots accumulate iron and can detect metal underground
- **Height:** 8-15 meters
- **Cultural Use:** BitterMarsh miners use root growths to prospect for ore deposits
- **Wood Properties:** Extremely heavy, sinks in water, resists even magical fire
