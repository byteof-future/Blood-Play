# Dripping Laurel (*Laurensia stillicida*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Leaves: Waxy, channel water to roots
- Flowers: White, fragrant, bloom in perpetual mist
- Ecology: Epiphyte host, provides nectar year-round
- Uses: Ornamental, aromatic oils, insect repellent
