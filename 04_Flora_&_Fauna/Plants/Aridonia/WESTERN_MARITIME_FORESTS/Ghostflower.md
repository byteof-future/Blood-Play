# Ghostflower (*Monotropa spectralae*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Type: Mycoheterotrophic (doesn't photosynthesize)
- Appearance: Pure white/pale pink, translucent
- Ecology: Grows on fungal networks connected to tree roots
- Rarity: Uncommon, appears after heavy rains
- Folklore: Said to mark sites of great magical power
