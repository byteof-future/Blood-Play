# Soul-Stealer (*Viscum vitae*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** All western forests
- **Form:** Parasitic shrub growing on tree branches
- **Berries:** White, sticky, poisonous
- **Cultural Uses:** 
- **Ecology:** Hemiparasitic (partially photosynthetic)

### Additional Info
- Mistletoe equivalent (winter festivals)
- Medicine: heart conditions (toxic in wrong doses)
- Druid ceremonies
