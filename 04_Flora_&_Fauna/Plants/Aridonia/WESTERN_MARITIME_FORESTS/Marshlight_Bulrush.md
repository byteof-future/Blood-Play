# Marshlight Bulrush (*Typha luminaris*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS

- **Distribution:** Throughout BitterMarsh wetlands
- **Characteristics:** Produces bioluminescent seed heads in autumn
- **Magical Properties:** Light attracts and entraps spirits
- **Height:** 2-3 meters
- **Ecology:** Provides nesting material for waterfowl
- **Folklore:** Travelers warned not to follow the lights at night
