# Nightshade Fern (*Pteridium nocturnum*)

- **Type:** Plant
- **Biome:** WESTERN_MARITIME_FORESTS


### Additional Info
- Fronds: Dark purple-black, unfurl at night
- Ecology: Shade specialist, spores spread by night creatures
- Properties: Mildly toxic, causes hallucinations in large doses
- Uses: Carefully prepared medicinal, ritual intoxicant
